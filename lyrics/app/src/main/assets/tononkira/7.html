FIHIRANA CEIM

ANDRIAMANITRA HE MAHAGAGA

1. Andriamanitra !
He mahagaga, ny zavatra nataon’ny tananao
Ny kintana sy kotrokoranandro
Ampisehoanao ny herinao

MIDERA ANAO ANDRIAMANITRA ô
FA LEHIBE, FA LEHIBE
MIDERA ANAO ANDRIAMANITRA ô
FA LEHIBE, FA LEHIBE

2. Raha mitsangatsangana ao an’ala
ka mahare hy hiram-borona
Sy rivotra mitsoka moramora
Ary mahita tendrombohitra

MIDERA ANAO ANDRIAMANITRA ô
FA LEHIBE, FA LEHIBE
MIDERA ANAO ANDRIAMANITRA ô
FA LEHIBE, FA LEHIBE


3. Raha tsaroako fa Jesoa Tompo
Natolotry ny Ray an-danitra
Ho avotra noho ny fahotako
Izay vita teo Gologota avokoa

MIDERA ANAO ANDRIAMANITRA ô
FA LEHIBE, FA LEHIBE

4. Ho tonga Jesosy Tompo soa
Hitondra ahy ho any an-danitra
Hiondrika aho sy hitsaoka azy
Andriamanitra, he fa lehibe

MIDERA ANAO ANDRIAMANITRA ô
FA LEHIBE, FA LEHIBE
