RAHA MBOLA IZY NO ANDRIAMANITRA

Ireo vavaka natao, ireo taraina sy talaho
Ireo antsonao andro aman’alina voarainy tsara
Na ireo fitsapana aza, ‘reo sedra maro tsy voalaza
Fantatray Tompo avokoa izay mety ahasoa
Ref.Hitany ireo odinao,
Ny masony mijery anao
Raha mbola izy no Andriamanitra aza matahotra

‘Reo toky nampanantenainy,
jereo fa tsy maintsy efainy
Rehefa nilaza izy ny hamonjy,
aoka hatoky
Tsarovy fa ilay Mpahary,
tsy manadino tsy manary
Raha mbola velona koa izy
aza mba kivy
Raha mbola izy no Andriamanitra
aza matahotra
